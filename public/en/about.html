ABOUT MHERO

The MHERO I (Chinese: Mengshi 917) is a full-size Luxury SUV manufactured by the Dongfeng Motor Corporation under the MHERO brand. The MHERO I which is the first vehicle under the MHERO brand, was introduced in August 2022 and launched the following year. It is also part of the Dongfeng Mengshi family of off-road vehicles.

MHERO TECHNOLOGY PARK

Welcome to MHERO Technology Park, a cutting-edge hub in the Wuhan Economic Development Zone. Spanning 485 acres with 96,000 square meters of advanced facilities, our park is dedicated to zero-carbon solutions and digital manufacturing. Featuring a world-leading smart factory, an advanced test center, an offroad experience base, and a test track, MHERO integrates innovation and sustainability to set a new benchmark in the new energy vehicle industry.

About Dongfeng Motor

Dongfeng Motor Corporation Limited is a significant backbone of China's automotive industry. Its predecessor, the Second Automobile Manufacturing Plant, was established in 1969. The company is headquartered in Wuhan, Hubei Province. It currently has total assets of 499.3 billion yuan and employs 127,000 people, ranked 188nd in the world's top 500. It has sold over 1,200,000 vehicles overseas, with its products sold in over 100 countries.

 

About Al Ghurair Group

For more than six decades, Al Ghurair Group has contributed to the KSA’s economic development. Built on trust, long-term vision, and strong local insight, the Group has established a diversified portfolio across sectors that support the nation’s continued progress.

Within its automotive portfolio, Al Ghurair Mobility represents leading international brands across the KSA. By combining global standards with regional understanding, the division delivers automotive solutions tailored to the needs of the local market.

Further strengthening this portfolio is MHERO, manufactured by Dongfeng Motor Corporation and distributed in the KSA by Al Ghurair Mobility. Designed to set a new benchmark in high-performance off-road SUVs, it brings advanced engineering together with the reassurance of an established nationwide service network.
