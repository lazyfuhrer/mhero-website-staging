CHALLENGING THE STATUS QUO

Conquering every terrain, the MHERO I reigns supreme. Seamlessly blending exceptional off-road mastery with one of a kind performance, this all-terrain SUV embodies adventure at its pinnacle. MHERO I embodies a Hybrid REV (Range-Extended Vehicle) technology in off-road electric cars, where it combines the power of an electric motor with a range extender, typically an internal combustion engine, to charge the battery. The REV version of the MHERO utilizes a 1.5-litre turbo petrol engine to power a battery that feeds energy to three electric motors, ensuring extended range and off-road capability.

All-Terrain Performance

UNSTOPPABLE TRACTION

The MHERO I is equipped with three differential locks. In challenging terrain, these locks ensure continuous traction for each wheel, guaranteeing the relentless progress of the MHERO I.

TOUGHER THAN GRANITE

The MHERO I excels beyond paved roads, setting new standards in off-road performance. Its rugged design ensures exceptional capability even in the toughest terrains, making every adventure an exhilarating experience.

REAR WHEEL INTELLIGENT DRIVING

With a minimum turning radius of 5.1 meters, the Rear Wheel Steering Assistance offers exceptional agility, making the MHERO I maneuverable in tight spaces and urban environments, despite its size.

CRAB MOVING MODE

Crab Moving Mode synchronizes the rear wheels with the front ones, allowing diagonal movement at low speeds, up to 10.6°. This feature enables the MHERO I to effortlessly navigate off-road trails or maneuver with ease in parking situations.

BREAKING BOUNDARIES

The MHERO I sets the bar for off-road excellence, conquering any terrain with all-wheel drive ease in climbing, water fording, and tilt angles. It's not just a vehicle; it's a symbol of relentless performance and uncompromising durability. Every path becomes an invitation to adventure, pushing the limits of what's possible off-road.

Fording Depth

The MHERO I confidently crosses waters with its heightened placement of the electric powertrain and electronics, combined with meticulous cabin sealing. It achieves an impressive maximum water fording depth of up to 90 cm.

Tilt Angle

Embrace adventure with the MHERO I – built for the bold. With exceptional stability up to a tilt angle of 40%, equivalent to 21,8°. It's a vehicle designed for those who dare to push the limits

Climbing Angle

The MHERO I achieves climbing capability of up to 100% on appropriate terrain, effortlessly conquering inclines of up to 45° when necessary. A testament to its unparalleled dominance during every expedition.

Passage Angle

The passage angle of the MHERO I on rugged terrain or when navigating larger ground undulations measures at 28°. This enables it to effortlessly conquer obstacles that prove challenging for many others.

Approach Angle

The approach angle defines the maximum incline that can be approached from flat ground without the vehicle's body overhangs touching the surface. Once again, the MHERO I demonstrates remarkable adaptability with values of 37° at the rear and front.

Ground Clearance

Ground clearance is crucial for off-road journeys, measuring th edistance from the ground to the vehicle's underside. TheMHERO I boasts aground clearance of up to 36 cm in off-road mode between the axles, showcasing its exceptional off-road capability.

MHERO Intelligent All-Terrain System

The MHERO Intelligent All-Terrain System significantly boosts the vehicle's traction, maneuverability, and stability when traversing off-road terrain. Drivers can effortlessly navigate through challenging landscapes, embracing the excitement of off-roading. With a robust all-wheel-drive system, six driver modes (Snow, Mud, Sand, Rock, Water, Auto), and additional specialized off-road driver assistance features, you're fully equipped for your next adventure.

With a commanding stance and undeniable presence, the MHERO I demands attention and turns heads wherever it roams. The Ultimate Design Statements

Strength, presence, and a boldly chiseled yet refined design: From every angle, the MHERO I exudes a commanding confidence. The side profile, enhanced by an array of edges and sharply defined shadow lines, intensifies this aesthetic. Combined with its wide-flared wheel arches, the MHERO I emerges as a trailblazing icon of off-road design.

Making a Bold Entrance

The commanding vehicle front is complemented by numerous edges and corners, as well as prominent air intakes on the hood. Striking LED headlights, adorned with a band of light, amplify the bold presence. The angular front logo, reminiscent of a stylized warrior mask while incorporating the 'M' from the brand name, further accentuates the vehicle's daring aesthetic.

A Departure with Presence

Edgy and functional, the MHERO I's rear design captivates with its prominent vertically stacked taillights and rearward-extending trunk extension. This extension not only adds to the vehicle's rugged aesthetic but also provides extra storage space, accommodating an additional 20 liters of cargo.

Rear Storage Box

The rear storage box provides ample space with a capacity of 20 liters, perfect for quick access to essentials such as charging cables and other items on the go. This convenient solution not only meets your practical needs but also incorporates a classic design element reminiscent of an off-road icon, traditionally housing a spare tire.

Front and Rear Sunroof

The MHERO I features two sunroofs, one for the front row and another for the rear seats, allowing ample natural light to flood the cabin and create an open and airy atmosphere. Additionally, they can be closed when necessary to prevent glare and provide shade, ensuring a comfortable driving experience in varying light conditions

Premium Cockpit

Experience the epitome of luxury in the MHERO I's premium cockpit. Crafted with fine wood, satin-finished steel, and sumptuous leather, every detail exudes sophistication. The intelligent interactive Cockpit features a multifunctional gear and driving mode selector, reminiscent of a private jet, while solid metal switches and buttons add a tactile feel.

Multimedia

Discover the MHERO I's advanced multimedia setup, featuring three expansive screens spanning coast to coast. The driver benefits from a 12.3-inch full LCD instrument panel and Head-Up Display, while a 15.6-inch central touchscreen offers intuitive controls. Passengers enjoy their own entertainment on a 12.3-inch screen. With 64-color ambient lighting and rearview mirror video streaming, every journey is elevated to new heights of comfort.

Driving Assistance

The MHERO I offers an extensive suite of advanced driver assistance systems, ensuring a comfortable and effortless driving experience. From Forward Collision Warning to Lane Departure Warning and All-Speed Adaptive Cruise Control, it provides comprehensive support for safety and convenience. Traffic Jam Assist and Highway Assist alleviate stress during congested urban traffic or long highway journeys.

Comfort & Climate

Indulge in unparalleled comfort with the MHERO's premium Nappa leather seats. The driver enjoys 16-way electric adjustment, while the passenger seat offers 14-way electric adjustment. Front seat memory allows you to save your preferred settings, while front seat heating, ventilation, and massage functions ensure maximum relaxation. Automatic air conditioning with an intelligent fragrance system and air purification guarantees a fresh cabin atmosphere.

Rear Seat Amenities

The touchscreen integrated into the rear armrest controls the rear seats, climate control, and multimedia, providing a comfortable and intelligent space in the rear row. Rear seats amenities include 8-way electric adjustment for the seat, including 4-way adjustment specifically for waist support, along with ISOFIX Child Safety seat interface. Additionally, rear seats feature heating, ventilation, and massage functions.

40-20-40 Split

The MHERO I offers versatile seating options with its 40-20-40 split configuration, allowing the rear seats to be adjusted to accommodate various cargo sizes while retaining seating capacity. With the convenience of electrically folding rear seats, adjusting the seating arrangement is effortless, ensuring seamless transitions between passenger and cargo modes. This flexible design ensures that the trunk volume can be expanded as needed without compromising on comfort or functionality.

LIMITLESS UTILITY

The MHERO I embodies versatility and utility as a dependable 5-seater, offering impressive payload and roof load capacity, towing capability, and generous luggage volume. With the option for a pre-mounted winch, it ensures readiness for various tasks and adventures without compromising on practicality. This all-round round talent is primed to conquer any challenge, for both everyday commutes and off-road adventures.
